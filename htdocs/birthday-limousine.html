BIRTHDAY LIMO SERVICE

Your big day is around the corner and this year you want to make it a special one? We have just what you need to top any party you've thrown before. Bring family & friends onto our limousine or stretch Hummer for a night nobody will forget.

Brewery tours, foodie frenzies, club hopping or shopping sprees are all on the menu. Start your night with dinner on Tybee and party your way to River Street. Just be careful, you might not want to get off the limo!

Frequently asked questions

Do you have to be 21+ to schedule a birthday on a limousine?
  • We service groups of all ages, and children have just as much fun as adults on our stretch SUV. Controlling the stereo & tv's as you make your way to the party, this will be an unforgettable birthday for your son or daughter.

Do you only serve Savannah, Georgia?
  • No! We serve all of south Georgia and even north Florida at times. Check out a list of our locations here, or call us today to find out if we can provide service!

How many people can fit on the stretch limo or stretch SUV?
  • We have many options depending on the size of your group. From 6-14 guests, we can accommodate. Each limo, SUV and van has a different price, so call now to chat with a representative.
​
How long can we rent the limousine in Savannah?
  • A minimum service requirement is 4 hours, but we are here to serve you as long as your night goes on! Pricing is based on hours. Call us to get a quote.
